What is My Profile?

The dropdown menu displayed with your name in the top-right navigation bar provides access to personal profile management features.

  • User - View and edit your own profile
  • Organization - View your organizations and switch between them
  • Language & Options - Change interface language and theme
  • Logout - Sign out of the system

User (Edit Profile)

Click User from the profile menu to open your profile edit page.

Options Tab

If you have the appropriate permissions, the Options tab allows managing organization assignments and RBAC roles. (See: Users Manual)

Change Password

There are 2 ways to change your password:

1. From the users list

In the users list table, click the (key) button on your row to change your password.

2. From the login screen (forgot password)
  • Click the forgot password link on the login screen
  • Enter your email address and submit the request
  • Reset your password via the link sent to your email

Organization

Click Organization from the profile menu to view your organizations.

Features
  • View all organizations you are assigned to
  • Each organization shows its name and logo
  • The currently active organization is highlighted with a blue border
Switch Organization

Click the Select button on any organization card to switch your active organization. This changes which organization context you are working in.

Users with the system_coder role can see and switch to all organizations. Regular users only see their assigned organizations.

Language & Options

Click Language & Options from the profile menu. A modal dialog will open.

Theme Selection
OptionDescription
Light modeLight background theme
Dark modeDark background theme

Theme preference is saved in the browser and persists across sessions.

Language Selection
  • All available system languages are listed with country flags
  • Select a language and click confirm to switch
  • The page will reload in the selected language
  • Your language preference is saved to your user profile

Logout

Click Logout from the profile menu to sign out.

  • Your authentication session (JWT token) is removed
  • You will be redirected to the home page
  • Language preference is preserved for next login
