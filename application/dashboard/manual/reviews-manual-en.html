What is Reviews?

Reviews is the Dashboard module for managing product reviews and ratings. You can view visitor star ratings (1-5) and written reviews, and delete inappropriate reviews.

Review features:

  • Star rating (1-5) - Visitors rate products with 1-5 stars
  • Written review - Text review alongside the star rating
  • Average rating - Average rating is automatically calculated per product
  • Spam protection - Honeypot, HMAC, rate limit, and Turnstile protection
  • Product-linked - Reviews depend on the product's comment=1 setting

View Product Reviews

Click a product from the list to view all its reviews.

Product Information

The top section shows the product summary:

  • Product title, photo
  • Price, publication status
Average Rating
  • Average star rating displayed prominently
  • Total number of reviews
Reviews List
  • Reviewer name and email
  • Star rating (1-5)
  • Written review text
  • Posted date

Delete Review

Click the delete (trash) button next to a review.

  • A confirmation dialog appears (SweetAlert2)
  • Soft delete - data is preserved, only deactivated

Website Reviews

Visitors can submit reviews on the product detail page:

  • Review form appears at the bottom of the product page
  • Visitors select stars (1-5), enter name, email (optional), and review text
  • Average star rating and review count are displayed on the products listing page
